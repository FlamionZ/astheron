What visual style for the new pages?

Should the subpages match the homepage's dense, animated feel — or take a different approach?

A

Same Energy — Full Animations

Every page gets the same glass-morphism panels, motion animations, parallax effects, and dark aesthetic as the homepage. Visually consistent but heavier to build and load.

B

Calm Interior — Animations on Entry Only

Pages share the same dark theme, fonts, and glass panels — but animations are limited to page entry transitions and scroll reveals. Content-focused, easier to read. The homepage remains the "wow" moment, subpages are where users get work done.

C

Mixed — Per Page Decision

Portfolio and Services get rich animations (they're visual). Blog, Careers, and Contact stay clean and readable. About gets moderate animation. You decide per page.
