How should the homepage and new pages relate?

This determines the overall site architecture

A

Keep Homepage + Add Subpages

The current scrollable homepage stays as the landing page. New pages (e.g., /services, /blog, /portfolio) are separate routes. Navbar updates with links to both anchor sections and new pages.

Structure:
/ → Current homepage (hero, services grid, products, testimonials, etc.)
/services → Detailed services page
/blog → Blog listing
/portfolio → Projects showcase
/about → Team & company story
/careers → Job listings
/contact → Full contact page
B

Slim Down Homepage + Move Content to Pages

Homepage becomes a shorter, punchier landing (hero + brief highlights + CTA). Detailed content moves to dedicated pages. Less scrolling on homepage, more focused subpages.

Structure:
/ → Slim hero + highlights + CTAs (no full services grid or testimonials)
/services → Full services detail (absorbs homepage services section)
/portfolio → Projects (absorbs case studies section)
/about → About + testimonials (absorbs about + testimonials sections)
Other pages same as option A
C

Full Multi-Page Site (No Scroll Homepage)

Complete restructure — homepage is just hero + navigation cards to each section. All content lives on dedicated pages. Traditional multi-page website feel.

Structure:
/ → Hero + navigation grid linking to all pages
All content distributed across subpages
No scrolling sections on homepage
